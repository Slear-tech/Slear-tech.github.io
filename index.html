CORON BAY

Coron Bay is a natural harbor located in the northern part of the Calamian Islands in Palawan, Philippines. It is known for its stunning landscapes of crystal-clear blue waters, limestone cliffs, and lush vegetation. The bay is a popular destination for diving and snorkeling, as it is home to numerous shipwrecks from World War II that have become artificial reefs and provide a unique underwater experience. In addition to diving, visitors can enjoy island hopping, swimming in the calm waters, and exploring nearby beaches. With its beautiful natural scenery and exciting activities, Coron Bay is a must-visit destination in Palawan.

EL NIDO

El Nido is a coastal town located in the northern part of Palawan, Philippines. It is known for its stunning beaches, clear blue waters, and dramatic limestone cliffs. El Nido is a popular destination for island hopping, which involves visiting nearby islands and exploring hidden lagoons, secret beaches, and snorkeling spots. The town is also home to a vibrant nightlife, with a range of bars and restaurants serving local and international cuisine. In addition to island hopping, visitors can also go kayaking, scuba diving, and hiking in the lush rainforest. With its natural beauty and range of activities, El Nido is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to Palawan.

Mount Tapyas

Mount Tapyas is a mountain located in Coron, Palawan, Philippines. It stands at 210 meters high and offers a stunning panoramic view of the town and the surrounding islands. Visitors can climb up the mountain's staircase, which has over 700 steps, to reach the top and enjoy the breathtaking view of the sunset. Along the way, there are small stations where visitors can take a rest, catch their breath, and take photos of the view. At the top of the mountain, visitors can also find a large metal cross, which is beautifully illuminated at night. Mount Tapyas is a popular destination in Coron and a must-visit for those looking for a stunning view and a bit of exercise.

TARAW CLIFF TRAIL

Taraw Cliff Trail is a popular hiking trail located in El Nido, Palawan, Philippines. The trail takes visitors on a challenging climb up the Taraw Peak, which stands at 230 meters high. The hike can be strenuous and requires a good level of fitness, but it rewards visitors with stunning panoramic views of El Nido and its surroundings. Along the way, visitors will need to navigate through limestone cliffs, sharp rocks, and steep drops, making the climb both thrilling and a little dangerous. At the top, visitors can enjoy the incredible view of Bacuit Bay and the surrounding islands. The Taraw Cliff Trail is a popular destination for adventure-seekers and hikers looking for a challenging climb and a unique view of El Nido.

PUERTO PRINCESA ZIPLINING

Puerto Princesa Ziplining is a thrilling adventure activity located in Puerto Princesa, Palawan, Philippines. The zipline is located at the Mitra Ranch, which is about 12 kilometers away from the city center. The zipline is 1.3 kilometers long and offers an exhilarating ride over lush green forests and scenic landscapes. Visitors can enjoy the breathtaking view of the surrounding mountains, forests, and sea while flying over the trees at high speed.

EL NIDO ZIPLINING

El Nido Ziplining is a popular adventure activity located in El Nido, Palawan, Philippines. The zipline is located in Las Cabanas Beach and takes visitors on a thrilling ride across a stunning tropical landscape. The zipline is 750 meters long and offers a bird's eye view of the beach, towering cliffs, and crystal-clear waters of the bay. Visitors can enjoy the thrill of flying at high speed while admiring the breathtaking natural scenery. The zipline is designed for all ages and is a must-try activity for anyone looking for adventure in El Nido.
